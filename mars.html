Mercury

Mercury, named after the Roman messenger of the gods due to its swift orbit around the Sun, is a captivating yet enigmatic planet in our solar system. It holds a unique status as the smallest and innermost planet, positioned just 36 million miles from the Sun, making it one of the hottest and most inhospitable worlds in our celestial neighborhood.
